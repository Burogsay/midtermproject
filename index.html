MY HOMETOWN DINGRAS

Dingras, officially the Municipality of Dingras (Ilocano: Ili ti Dingras; Filipino: Bayan ng Dingras), is a 2nd class municipality in the province of Ilocos Norte, Philippines.
According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 40,127 people distributed over 31 barangays.
